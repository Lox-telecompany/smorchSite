About myself
Greetings, my name is Alexey Nikolaevich. I grew passionate about photography in the very youth. Like many young men, I used to sit in the dark room developing films and making prints. I was carried away by the process, full of intriguing discoveries and embarrassing mistakes. It made me realize that my interest was focused on people – for their individual uniqueness, and nature – that offered infinite beauty of locations and unpredictable manifestation of its marvel. Inspiring encounters and impressions leave a definite trace making life full of bright colors. And it is from photography that I learned how to see and appreciate this.
About photography
Yousuf Karsh, one of the greatest portrait photographers of the 20th century, said that “the heart and mind are the true lens of the camera”. Possessing top-grade equipment is not as important as the skill to use what you have at hand, as the ability to see marvel in everyday life, anticipate and capture the right moment. Photography is a tool that enables you not only to reveal something new to the world, but to take a look deep inside of yourself, make a step towards new discoveries, and catch the Ever-escaping.
